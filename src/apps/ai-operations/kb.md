# Knowledge Base Definition: AI Site Operations

## Definition
The AI Site Operations module is a multi-functional tool for e-commerce management. It automates routine tasks like content creation, SEO optimization, and product data entry.

## Boundaries
- **Input**: Store URLs, product links, marketing topics, ad account credentials.
- **Output**: Optimized product titles/descriptions, enhanced images, blog posts, ad performance reports.
- **Exclusions**: Does not handle physical fulfillment or logistics (focused on digital operations and marketing).

## File Logic
1. **Overview**: Displays traffic and conversion data from connected stores.
2. **Optimization**: AI analyzes product listings and suggests SEO improvements.
3. **Ad Management**: Integrates with TikTok/FB/Google Ads for performance monitoring and AI-driven budget suggestions.
4. **Scrape**: AI-powered product ingestion from source URLs (e.g., 1688, Amazon).
5. **Blog Writer**: Generates SEO-optimized blog posts using the global knowledge base.

## AI Recognition Hints
- Focus on ROI and conversion rate optimization in all suggestions.
- Ensure all generated content (titles, descriptions, blogs) is SEO-friendly and matches the brand's voice.
- Use data-driven insights for ad budget and asset recommendations.
